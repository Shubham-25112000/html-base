Google Ads & PPC Campaigns

üéØ Creating Your First Google Ad Campaign

  1. Define Objectives: What do you want to achieve? (Sales, leads, awareness)
  2. Keyword Research: Find relevant search terms your audience uses
  3. Create Ad Groups: Group similar keywords together
  4. Write Compelling Ads: Headlines, descriptions, and call-to-actions
  5. Set Bidding Strategy: How much you'll pay per click
  6. Define Budget: Daily or campaign budget limits
  7. Launch & Monitor: Track performance and optimize
